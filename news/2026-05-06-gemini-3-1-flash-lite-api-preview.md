Google launched Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite in developer preview via the Gemini API in Google AI Studio and on Vertex AI. It is priced at $0.25 per million input tokens and $1.50 per million output tokens, making it one of the cheapest capable models available. According to Artificial Analysis benchmarks, the model delivers 2.5× faster time-to-first-token and 45% higher output throughput than Gemini 2.5 Flash, while maintaining similar or better quality on reasoning tasks.

The model is designed for high-volume developer workloads — tasks like translation, content moderation, UI generation, and simulation — where latency and cost per token matter more than pushing benchmarks. Gemini 3.1 Pro, which sits at the top of Google's current model lineup, scored 94.3% on GPQA Diamond and continues to lead on reasoning benchmarks.

For developers building production applications on tight cost budgets, Flash-Lite's combination of speed and pricing makes it the most accessible tier of the Gemini 3 family yet.
